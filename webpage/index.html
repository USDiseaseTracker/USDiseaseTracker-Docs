About the Project

The US Disease Tracker project aims to provide consolidated epidemiologically sound data, analytics, and insights for monitoring and responding to disease threats across the United States. This repository establishes the processes, standards, and data formats that enable construction of a consolidated database and dashboard to track infectious diseases in real-time.
